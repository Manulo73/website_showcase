Who are we?

We are an American-Singaporean multinational technology company that designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, financial services, and gaming hardware. The triple-headed snake trademark of Rozer is one of the most recognized logos in the global gaming and esports communities. With a fan base that spans every continent, the company has designed and built the world’s largest gamer-focused ecosystem of hardware, software and services.
